About Me

Tera and Me

Tera and I met in 2020 and got married in 2022. She has been one of the biggest blessings in my life, and I’m grateful for the life we’ve built together.

We both work from home, so we get to spend a lot of everyday life together, usually with a couple of dogs nearby keeping us company. Outside of work, we enjoy getting out, making memories, and just having fun together.

One of our favorite things to do is go to concerts, and over the past five years we’ve probably been to more than fifty. We also enjoy country dancing at local dancehalls and finding reasons to get out and enjoy life together.

Our Dogs

Keo

Keo is a Chaweeny and one of our full-time office mates. He is about seven years old and definitely does his part to keep the house interesting during the workday.

Raider

Raider is a Chihuahua and the other member of the home office team. He is about nine years old and, like Keo, takes his role as office companion very seriously.

Tennis

I’ve been playing tennis for about the past ten years and have traveled all over Texas to play in tournaments. I’m not going to pretend I’m some elite player, but I’m very thankful the sport has levels for people like me.

Tennis has given me a lot of great experiences over the years. Two of the coolest were getting to meet John Newcombe at his tennis ranch and getting to see Venus Williams play in person.

More than anything, it has been a fun challenge, a great outlet, and a hobby that has brought a lot of good memories.
