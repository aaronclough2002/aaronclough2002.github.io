About Me

A little more about the people, hobbies, and everyday life that matter most to me outside of work.

Tera and Me

Tera and I met in 2020 and got married in 2022. She has been one of the biggest blessings in my life, and I’m genuinely grateful for the life we’ve built together.

Since we both work from home, we get to share a lot of the ordinary parts of life together too, usually with Raider and Keo nearby acting like honorary office assistants.

Outside of work, we enjoy getting out and making memories together. Over the past five years, we’ve probably been to more than fifty concerts, and we also love country dancing at local dancehalls.

Our Dogs

Keo

Keo is a Chaweeny and one of our full-time office mates. He’s about seven years old and definitely does his part to keep the house interesting during the workday.

Raider

Raider is a Chihuahua and the other member of the home office team. He’s about nine years old and, like Keo, takes his role as office companion very seriously.

Tennis

I’ve been playing tennis for about the past ten years and have traveled all over Texas to play in tournaments. I’m not going to pretend I’m some elite player, but I’m very thankful the sport has levels for people like me.

Tennis has given me a lot of great experiences over the years. Two of the coolest were getting to meet John Newcombe at his tennis ranch and getting to see Venus Williams play in person.

More than anything, it has been a fun challenge, a great outlet, and a hobby that has brought a lot of good memories.
