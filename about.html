Tennis

I’ve been playing tennis for about the past ten years and have traveled all over Texas to play in tournaments. I’m not going to pretend I’m some elite player, but I am very thankful the sport has tournament levels for players like me.

Tennis has given me the chance to compete, improve, and enjoy a lot of great experiences along the way. Two of the coolest moments have been getting to meet John Newcombe at his tennis ranch and getting to see Venus Williams play in person.

More than anything, tennis has been a fun challenge and a great way to spend time, stay active, and make memories over the years.
